Splash

Industrial Design - Northwestern University

Problem

For this assignment, we were asked to create a new speaker that could be used on the go.

Pain Points

Portable speakers often do not have a full enough sound when placed in a room, so I wanted to design something that would work on the go while still being exciting at home.

There is also a lot of unutilized space on speakers that could be used to differentiate them.

Thumbnail drawings of some initial concepts.

Solution

My final concept was a two-piece speaker, with a part that remains in whichever room in which the speaker is used at home, and a portable piece one can take with them.

  • Light Frame The metal light frame attached to this speaker plays light synchronized with the music.

  • Portability A button underneath the handle allows a portion of the speaker to be removed and taken on the go. This piece would charge when placed into the home unit.

  • Battery Status The light frame will flash red intermittently when the battery is low.
