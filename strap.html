Strap

Industrial Design - Northwestern University

Problem

Backpacks for athletic teams at Northwestern do not feel unique to Northwestern or to the teams. We looked to create new, team-specific bags teams could wear with pride on campus.

Pain Points

As a member of the Evanston boxing club, I chose to work on a boxing bag. Northwestern and Under Armour both have distinct styles, and I attempted to incorporate both into a backpack that was inspired by boxing.

Initial thumbnails.

First Attempt

My original solution was inspired by boxing mitts. After working on this idea for a while, I decided it was too generic and would not stand out enough as a boxing backpack. So I returned to the drawing board.

Solution

My final sketch was based on a double-end bag and incorporated elements of boxing equipment, such as mesh and leather straps.

  • Gloves Pouch Boxing gloves never smell good, so I added in a pouch where they could both fit and be aired out.

  • Wildcard Holder Northwestern student IDs are used for access to most buildings on campus, so a place where they can be easily accessed means students can get where they are going quicker.
