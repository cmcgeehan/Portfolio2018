Stop

Industrial Design - Northwestern University

Problem

For this assignment, we were tasked with constructing a new bus stop on campus more in tune with Northwestern's design style and more practical for student use.

Pain Points

Based on discussion with Northwestern students, the largest source of discomfort for students was lack of protection for the elements. Another thing students said they would appreciate was an indication - from a distance - that a bus is arriving shortly.

Thumbnail drawings of some initial concepts.

Solution

I designed a bus stop that relies on many of the materials common to new buildings at Northwestern - like stone, metal and glass - to craft a bus stop that attempts to solve the problems referenced by the students with whom I discussed this problem.

  • Arrival Light The purple light at the top of the lighthouse-esque tower will alert students to arriving buses.

  • Overhang The glass above the bench serves as protection from the rain and snow.

  • Accessibility No bus stop on campus provides a place to sit and rest. I added a bench so people who cannot or do not want to stand until the bus arrives will have somewhere to rest.
