Sturdy

Industrial Design - Northwestern University

Problem

For the first assignment for this class, we sought to design a reimagined box cutter for construction workers.

Pain Points

Standard Xacto knives do not look or feel durable. Construction workers require tools that will withstand heavy use as well as dropping.

A drawing of a standard Xacto knife.

Solution

My final sketch used the rubberized look of many construction tools, with grip underneath for easier use.

  • Open Switch The switch to open the blade has grip and is a little inset, to make it easier to open and close, even when wearing thick gloves.

  • Belt Clip The belt clip allows construction workers to keep it on them so it's easy to access when they need it.

  • Rope Cutter I added a rope cutter underneath the blade for use around the construction site.

  • Blade Holder The button on the side releases extra blade storage, in case case more are needed in a pinch.
